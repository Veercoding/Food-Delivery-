AboutUs

Why Choose us?

Strathmore is revolutionizing the way you experience food delivery. Founded in 2022 by a team of tech enthusiasts and foodies, we're on a mission to connect hungry customers with the best local restaurants, faster than ever before. Our Story: Born from the frustration of cold takeout and long wait times, Strathmore combines cutting-edge technology with a passion for great food. We've built a network of dedicated drivers and partnered with top-rated restaurants to ensure your meals arrive hot, fresh, and on time.
